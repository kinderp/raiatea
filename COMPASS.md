# COMPASS

> Raiatea does not seek to answer every question. It seeks to help people find their bearings.

## Purpose

This document preserves the direction of Raiatea while its concepts, architecture, and implementations evolve.

## Foundational principles

1. **No information without provenance.** Every meaningful statement must remain traceable to its sources and transformations.
2. **AI is not a primary source.** AI may interpret, compare, transform, and explain; it must not erase the distinction between evidence and synthesis.
3. **No knowledge without time and context.** Facts, claims, opinions, predictions, and interpretations must retain when, where, and by whom they were expressed.
4. **Understanding before automation.** We first learn how a useful process works through real cases; only then do we automate it.
5. **Uncertainty must remain visible.** Confidence, disagreement, missing evidence, and unresolved questions are part of knowledge.
6. **The journey matters more than the race.** Raiatea is built for durable understanding, not for chasing every technological fashion.
7. **The project must outlive its tools.** Its knowledge, decisions, and formats must remain usable independently of any single model, provider, platform, or subscription.
8. **Every product shares one navigable memory.** Research Notebook, Learning Lab, field intelligence, personal knowledge, and future products should reuse a common core without losing their distinct purposes.
9. **Human judgment remains accountable.** Automation may propose; people must be able to inspect, challenge, correct, and reverse consequential decisions.
10. **We leave a route others can verify.** Documents, experiments, claims, and architectural choices must be understandable without relying on private conversations.

## Guiding question

> How can a person navigate an ocean of continuously evolving knowledge without losing curiosity, context, and the ability to understand?

## Decision test

Before adding a feature, source, model, or abstraction, ask:

> Does this help someone navigate knowledge more accurately, deeply, or independently?

If the answer is unclear, the decision remains open.
